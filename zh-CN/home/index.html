Key Features

The key features of StreamPark™ is to make your stream processing easier.

Development Framework

Provides a framework to simplify Apache Flink® and Apache Spark™ job development, with plug-and-play connectors that reduce the learning curve and development complexity, improving efficiency.

Streaming Platform

Provides a streaming job operation and management platform. It covers the entire lifecycle — from development and build, deployment and launch, state management and maintenance.

Unified Batch & Streaming

Supports both Apache Flink® and Apache Spark™, enabling seamless integration of streaming and batch processing, with multi-engine and multi-version support on a single platform.

Rich Ecosystem

Integrates with a variety of open-source tools, including Flink®, Spark™, Paimon™, Doris, and Flink CDC, offering users greater flexibility in technology choices.

Easy to use

Apache StreamPark™ is designed to reduce the learning curve and entry barrier. With just one service, it is easy to deploy. Even beginners can begin within minutes.

Mature and Stable

Widely deployed in large-scale production environments, offering second-level job state tracking, real-time monitoring, and alert notifications to ensure stability and support ongoing project growth.

Development Framework

StreamPark™ provided a development framework to quickly develop stream processing applications.
